About

I have been programming as long as I can remember. In highschool I started out programming Arduino boards and robotics, deciding I wanted to to go to school for computer science.

I went on to get my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at University of Denver in Colorado, and an internship in Centennial following graduation. I got my first taste of frontend development, working with Angular and CSS.

In 2018, I moved to Paris, France to work at Shift Technology as full stack engineer- working in React and C#. At the start of the pandemic, I moved back home to Dallas Texas.

Since then, I have been working remotely from Dallas and taking on side projects to design and implement web-applications.
